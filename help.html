1. Getting Started — Login & First Password

▼
1
Open the portal
Go to: https://cuepointe.github.io/UBF-Request-Portal/
Bookmark this address so you can always find it.
2
Enter your UBF work email
Use your official email address e.g. s.abonyo@ugandabiodiversityfund.org
3
First time — enter default password
Type admin as your password. You will be immediately asked to set your own private password.
4
Set your own password
Choose a password of at least 8 characters. You will use this every time you log in.
Passwords expire every 90 days — you will be prompted to set a new one automatically.
Never share your password with anyone. If you forget it, contact Tom Otieno: t.otieno@ugandabiodiversityfund.org

2. The Dashboard

▼
After login you land on the Dashboard. This is your home page showing all your submissions.
A
Stat Cards
The coloured cards at the top show how many submissions are at each stage: Pending, Prepared, Reviewed, Cleared, Approved, Rejected.
B
Submissions Table
Every submission you can see is listed here. Click any Ref No (shown in blue) to open the full record.
C
Filters
Use the Status filter and Search bar to find specific records quickly.
Package indicator: A green pkg:2 badge next to a Ref No means that record has 2 additional forms attached to its package.

3. Submitting a Request (with Package)

▼
All Staff
1
Click "Request for Goods/Services" on the Dashboard
Fill in all required fields marked with a red asterisk *
2
Add more forms if needed (Optional)
Scroll down to the "Add Forms to This Package" section. Click "+ Travel Business Plan" or "+ Accountability Form" to expand and fill those forms on the same page. You can add both.
3
Attach supporting documents
Use the Attachments section to upload quotations, approvals or any supporting files.
4
Click "Submit Package"
All forms are submitted together as one package. You will see a green confirmation message with your Ref No.
After submission the record goes to Susan Abonyo (Admin Officer) for preparation. You cannot edit it unless it is rejected back to you.

5. Adding Forms to an Existing Package

▼
All Staff & Management
Any user can add more forms to any record at any stage before final approval.
1
Click the Ref No on the Dashboard to open the record
The full record opens in a popup window.
2
Find "Add Forms to This Package" at the top
You will see 6 expandable panels: Travel, Accountability, Evaluation, LPO, GRN, Payment Voucher.
3
Click any panel to expand it
The form fields appear inline. Fill in the required information. Use "+ Add Row" buttons on tables to add more rows as needed.
4
Click "Save & Add to Package"
The form is saved instantly and linked to this package. You will see it appear in the Package section below.
5
Repeat for as many forms as needed
There is no limit. Add all necessary forms before forwarding to the next approver.
All forms added to a package travel together through the entire approval chain. Every approver sees the complete package.

6. Attaching Files & Receipts

▼
All Staff
1
Open any record from the Dashboard
2
Find "Attach Documents / Receipts to This Package"
This section is always visible at the top of every record.
3
Click "Choose Files" and select your documents
You can select multiple files at once. Accepted formats: PDF, Word, Excel, JPG, PNG.
4
Click "Upload & Attach to Record"
Files are uploaded and permanently attached. They appear in the Attachments section with View and Download buttons.
Attached files are visible to everyone in the approval chain. Superiors can also download them directly.

7. Downloading & Printing Forms

▼
All Staff
1
Open any record from the Dashboard
2
Use the action buttons at the top of the record
Download This Form (PDF) — downloads just this one form as a PDF file
Download Complete Package (PDF) — downloads ALL linked forms together as one PDF
Print — opens a print-ready view of the form
When downloading PDF make sure your internet is stable. The PDF library loads from the internet the first time. Wait a few seconds after clicking.
3
Download individual attachments
In the Attachments section, each file has a View and Download button.

8. Comments & Communication

▼
All Staff
1
Open any record and scroll to Comments
2
Type your comment and click "Post Comment"
Your comment is permanently recorded with your name, role and timestamp.
3
Reply to a comment
Click "Reply" below any comment, type your response and click "Post Reply".
Comments cannot be deleted once posted. They are part of the permanent audit trail.
Management Notes (Admin Officer, FAM, ED only): A separate "Add Note" section allows managers to add official instructions visible to all parties.

9. Document Archive

▼
FAMAdmin OfficerED
All packages approved by the ED are automatically saved to the Archive. This is your auditor-ready file cabinet.
1
Click "Archive" in the navigation menu
2
Create folders (FAM only)
Click "+ New Folder", type a name e.g. "Q1 2026 Procurement" and press OK. Folders help organise files by quarter, project or category.
3
Move files from Unfiled to a folder (FAM only)
Newly approved files appear in the Unfiled section. Click "Move to Folder" on any file and select the target folder.
4
Rename files and folders
Click "Rename" next to any file or folder to give it a meaningful name for easy retrieval.
5
Download complete packages
Click "Download Package" on any archived file to get a PDF of all forms and documents in that package.
The Archive is permanent. Files are never deleted. This is what auditors will review.

12. Troubleshooting

▼
?
I cannot log in
Check your email spelling. Make sure you are using your UBF work email. If you forgot your password contact Tom Otieno.
?
I submitted a form but cannot see it
Refresh the page with Ctrl+Shift+R. If still not visible check the History page.
?
The PDF downloads but opens blank
Make sure you have a stable internet connection. The PDF library loads from the internet. Wait 3 seconds after clicking then try again.
?
I see a "Data conflict" error
Someone else saved data at the same moment. Refresh the page and try again.
?
My session expired
Sessions expire after 8 hours. Simply log in again. Your data is safe.
?
I cannot see management forms (Evaluation, LPO etc.)
These forms are only available to Admin Officer, FAM and ED. Contact your supervisor if you need access.
